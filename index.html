The Recipe Website You Always Dreamed Of

Let's be honest, the last time you looked for a recipe online, you got really frustrated with what you found. Chef Jylix is here to end all of the pain and misery caused by other recipe websites!

Right To The Point

By far the worst thing about most recipe websites is the fact that before you get to any actual recipes, there is this massive blog-post you have to read through (or scroll through more likely).

That irritation is a thing of the past! Chef Jylix puts the fun facts and stories after the instructions in every recipe, ensuring that you have no trouble finding the information you are looking for.

Simple Ingredients

How many times have you had to look up the name of an ingredient you saw on a recipe website? You won't have to do that here! Our recipes use the simplest ingredients, most of them coming right out of a can you can pull off the shelf of your local grocery store.

Customized To Your Liking (Coming Soon)

Set up an account and select your preferences, and Chef Jylix will automatically replace the listed ingredients with the substitute that works best for you! Gone are the days of searching for that low-carb version or dairy-free alternative. Let us do all the work!
