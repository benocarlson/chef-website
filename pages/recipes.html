Peruvian Potatoes

A south-american style stew typically served with rice

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 lb. Ground Beef
  • 1/2 Medium Onion, diced
  • 4 Medium Potatoes, diced
  • 1 Can Diced Tomatoes
  • Salt, Pepper, Garlic, Oregano
  • Ground Hot Pepper (ptional)

Instructions:

Brown the beef with the onion. Add the potatoes and the diced tomatoes. Add the spices. Mix thoroughly. Put a lid over the pot and stew, mixing every few minutes to prevent burning. Once the potatoes are tender, remove from heat.

Serve over rice with cheese and sour cream.

Paprika Chicken

A hungarian style pasta

Ingredients:

  • 1 Small Onion, diced
  • 2 TBS Butter
  • 1 Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast
  • 1 TBS Paprika
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 cup Sour Cream
  • 1 lb. Dry Noodles

Instructions:

Fill a large pot with water and add a dash of salt. Put the pot on the stove on high to bring the water to a boil. Boil the noodles until cooked.

In another pan, melt the butter and sautee the onion in it. Add the chicken and sautee until the chicken is entirely cooked. Remove from heat and add the paprika, salt, and sour cream.

Combine cooked noodles and creamy chicken mixture.
