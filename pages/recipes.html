Peruvian Potatoes

A south-american style stew typically served with rice

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 lb. Ground Beef
  • 1/2 Medium Onion, diced
  • 4 Medium Potatoes, diced
  • 1 Can Diced Tomatoes
  • Salt, Pepper, Garlic, Oregano
  • Ground Hot Pepper (ptional)

Instructions:

Brown the beef with the onion. Add the potatoes and the diced tomatoes. Add the spices. Mix thoroughly. Put a lid over the pot and stew, mixing every few minutes to prevent burning. Once the potatoes are tender, remove from heat.

Serve over rice with cheese and sour cream.
